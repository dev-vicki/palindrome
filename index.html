Is your Birthday Palindrome or not ?
This app checks your birthdate in 6 formats yyyy-mm-dd, dd-mm-yyyy, mm-dd-yyyy, dd-mm-yy, mm-dd-yy, yy-mm-dd e.g. if your birthdate is 07 Aug 1999, then app will check for 07081999, 08071999, 19990807, 070899, 080799, 990807.
